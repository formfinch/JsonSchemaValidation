## FAQ

### Can I evaluate the library before purchasing?

Yes. You can use the library freely for evaluation, testing, and proof-of-concept work. A commercial license is only required when you deploy to production or use it in a commercial context.

### Do I need a license for each project?

No. Commercial licenses are per-developer or per-team, not per-project. Once licensed, you can use the library in unlimited projects.

### What if I'm building an open source project?

If your open source project is non-commercial (not generating revenue, not backed by a commercial entity), you can use the PolyForm Noncommercial License. If your open source project is commercially backed or monetized, you need a commercial license.

### Can I redistribute the library in my commercial product?

Yes, with a commercial license. You may include the library in your commercial products for distribution to your customers.
